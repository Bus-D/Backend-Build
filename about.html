About Bus-D Designs

Freelance UI/UX & Web Designer focused on clarity, usability, and thoughtful design

Hi, I’m Brandon Garrett — a freelance UI/UX and web designer behind Bus-D Designs. I specialize in designing clean, intuitive interfaces for websites, web apps, and mobile apps, with a strong focus on usability and real-world user experience.

I’m currently studying graphic design and web development, and I use my freelance work as a way to gain hands-on experience while building products that solve real problems. Every project is approached with a balance of visual design, structure, and user-focused thinking.

My design process emphasizes clarity, consistency, and purpose. I focus on understanding user needs, defining clear flows, and creating interface systems that are easy to use and ready for development. I design primarily in Figma and collaborate closely with clients to ensure each project meets both user and business goals.

This website was fully designed and coded by me, and it continues to evolve as I refine my skills in UI/UX design, accessibility, and front-end development. I’m always looking to improve and take on projects that challenge me creatively and technically.

If you’re looking for a freelance UI/UX designer who values thoughtful design, clear communication, and practical solutions, I’d love to work with you.
