How I Work

A simple, collaborative design process from concept to handoff

1. Discovery

We define goals, users, and requirements through discussion, research, and feature mapping to align the project from the start.

2. Wireframes

Low-fidelity layouts establish structure, navigation, and content flow before visual design begins.

3. UI Design

High-fidelity screens focused on hierarchy, typography, spacing, and consistency.

4. Prototype

Interactive prototypes for testing, stakeholder feedback, and development planning.

5. Handoff

Developer-ready files, components, and documentation for a smooth transition into build and launch.
